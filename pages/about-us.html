About Us

At Logo, we believe in the transformative power of effective CRM strategies. Founded in 2023, we've dedicated ourselves to helping businesses unlock the full potential of Salesforce.

A partnership rooted in mutual growth, technological excellence, and a shared vision for the future. Join us, and let's co-create your Salesforce success story.
